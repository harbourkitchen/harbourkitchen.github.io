About Harbour Kitchen

Harbour Kitchen is Melbourne’s premier function venue located in Docklands. With floor-to-ceiling windows showcasing stunning water views, it’s the perfect destination for both private parties and corporate events. Whether you're celebrating a birthday or hosting a company gathering, Harbour Kitchen brings elegance and flexibility to every occasion.
